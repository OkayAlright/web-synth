Explanation:

This web page is a demo/experiment to play around with the Web Audio API. Consider this a "V0.2.0". You can only control waveform, gain and pitch. Hopefully I (may) add filters, and modulators, in the near future. Better mobile compatibility will also be a goal.

Contact me at ldavis@marlboro.edu or check out my repo.

Changes in V0.2.0: Added two more oscillators.
